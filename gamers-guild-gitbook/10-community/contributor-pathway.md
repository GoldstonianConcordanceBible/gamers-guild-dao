# Contributor Pathway

The community should make it easy for people to move from observer to contributor.

## Why This Matters

Many people want to help but do not know how.

A contributor pathway solves that.

## Example Pathway

### Visitor
A person who is learning what GGUILD is.

### Member
A person who joins the community and follows updates.

### Participant
A person who engages in discussions, shares ideas, or supports the ecosystem.

### Contributor
A person who creates content, helps with research, moderates, builds, or improves the system.

### Core Contributor
A person who contributes consistently and helps move the project forward.

## What Contribution Can Look Like

Contribution may include:

- content creation
- community support
- documentation help
- research
- moderation
- governance support
- technical work

## Rule

A strong pathway gives people a visible way to grow inside the ecosystem.
