# Community Standards

GGUILD should feel competitive, creative, and structured.

That starts with clear community standards.

## Core Standard

The community should be:

- respectful
- focused
- constructive
- contribution-aware
- mission-aligned

## What We Encourage

We encourage:

- thoughtful discussion
- helpful questions
- real contribution
- project-building energy
- community support
- curiosity and learning

## What We Avoid

We avoid:

- spam
- constant price-only discussion
- harassment
- confusion through careless messaging
- low-effort noise
- behavior that weakens trust

## Why This Matters

A strong community is not just active.

It is readable, healthy, and aligned.

## Rule

GGUILD should be a place where people can build, learn, contribute, and grow without the community collapsing into chaos.
