# From Visitor to Core Member

A healthy ecosystem gives people a way to grow into deeper roles over time.

## Stage 1 — Visitor

The visitor is learning what GGUILD is and deciding if it feels real.

At this stage, clarity matters most.

## Stage 2 — Member

The member joins channels, reads updates, and begins to understand the system.

At this stage, trust matters most.

## Stage 3 — Participant

The participant starts engaging in conversations, asking questions, and following progress.

At this stage, belonging matters most.

## Stage 4 — Contributor

The contributor starts creating value through content, organization, research, moderation, or system support.

At this stage, recognition matters most.

## Stage 5 — Core Member

The core member contributes consistently and helps shape the culture and future of the project.

At this stage, responsibility matters most.

## Why This Matters

People stay longer when they can see where they fit and how they can grow.
