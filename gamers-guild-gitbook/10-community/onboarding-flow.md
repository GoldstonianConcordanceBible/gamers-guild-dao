# Onboarding Flow

When someone joins GGUILD, they should know where to start.

## Why This Matters

Most communities lose people because new members feel lost.

A simple onboarding flow fixes that.

## Basic Onboarding Flow

### Step 1
New member joins Discord, Telegram, or finds the project through Pump.fun, X, GitHub, or GitBook.

### Step 2
They are directed to the Start Here section of the GitBook.

### Step 3
They read:

- What is GGUILD
- Why This Is Legit
- Overview
- Token Utility

### Step 4
They enter the community with a basic understanding of the mission.

### Step 5
They choose how they want to participate:

- learn
- contribute
- create
- support
- build

## Best Practice

Do not assume people understand the system automatically.

Point them to the docs first.

## Goal

The goal of onboarding is simple:

turn confusion into clarity.
