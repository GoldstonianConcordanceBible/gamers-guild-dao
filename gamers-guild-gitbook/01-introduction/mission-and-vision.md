# Mission and Vision

## Mission

The mission of Gamers Guild Token is to build a decentralized gaming economy that rewards meaningful contribution, strengthens community governance, and demonstrates how gaming ecosystems can be organized through transparent digital infrastructure.

## Vision

The long-term vision is to create a modular gaming coordination layer where tokens, AI agents, reputation systems, and community governance work together to support:

- gamers
- streamers
- guild leaders
- tournament organizers
- creators
- educators
- digital communities

GGUILD is designed to become more than a token. It is intended to become an ecosystem layer that can support a range of gaming-related activities, from content rewards and tournament coordination to community governance and digital identity.

## SydTek University Connection

Because this project is developed as a SydTek University Sub-DAO case study, the mission includes an educational dimension. Every major design choice can be studied, documented, assessed, and improved through a formal academic framework.

This creates a rare dynamic: the token is both a system and a classroom.

Students are not only learning about tokenization and DAO design. They are analyzing and shaping a real operating model.
