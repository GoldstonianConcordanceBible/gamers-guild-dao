# Project Bio

This page gives you short and long versions of the GGUILD project bio for profiles, docs, and public platforms.

## Short Bio

Gamers Guild Token (GGUILD) is a gaming-native utility and governance token developed as a SydTek University Sub-DAO case study focused on creator rewards, community participation, AI-powered reputation, and the future of gaming economies.

## Medium Bio

Gamers Guild Token (GGUILD) is a utility-oriented token ecosystem built for gaming communities, creator incentives, governance participation, and AI-supported digital coordination. Developed within the SydTek University ecosystem, GGUILD serves as both a live community system and a case study for esports, gaming, Web3, and AI-integrated program development.

## Long Bio

Gamers Guild Token (GGUILD) is a gaming-native utility and governance token designed to support community participation, contributor rewards, creator incentives, governance systems, and AI-assisted reputation across gaming ecosystems. Built as a Sub-DAO case study within SydTek University, GGUILD is more than a token launch. It is a documented digital economy experiment and an academic bridge between gaming communities, decentralized systems, AI-enabled operations, and higher education.
