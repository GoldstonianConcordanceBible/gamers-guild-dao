# Tagline and Positioning

This page defines the clearest public-facing identity for GGUILD.

## Core Tagline

GGUILD is a gaming-native utility and governance token developed as a SydTek University Sub-DAO case study.

## Alternate Taglines

- Built for gamers. Structured for communities.
- A better digital economy for gaming communities.
- Gaming, governance, AI, and community in one system.
- A live gaming economy case study built at SydTek University.

## Positioning Statement

GGUILD is designed to support gaming communities through:

- contributor rewards
- governance participation
- creator incentives
- AI-supported reputation systems
- community-centered digital coordination

## Positioning Rule

The message should always sound:

- clear
- serious
- structured
- utility-focused
- community-centered

## What To Emphasize

Emphasize:

- gaming use cases
- SydTek University connection
- governance and participation
- contributor rewards
- documentation and structure

## What Not To Emphasize

Do not emphasize:

- price predictions
- fast upside
- hype language
- vague promises
- speculation as the main story
