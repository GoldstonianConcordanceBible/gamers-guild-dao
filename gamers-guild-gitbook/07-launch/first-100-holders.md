# First 100 Holders Plan

The first 100 holders shape the culture of the project.

This stage matters more than scale.

## Objective

Find the first 100 people who are aligned with the mission, not just chasing fast upside.

## Ideal Early Members

The first community should include people such as:

- gamers
- creators
- streamers
- esports students
- DAO-curious builders
- moderators
- educators
- friends of the SydTek ecosystem

## Where They Come From

Potential early holders may come from:

- existing gaming contacts
- SydTek University audience
- YouTube audience
- X followers
- Discord communities
- creator collaborators

## What They Need To See

Before joining, they should be able to understand:

- what GGUILD is
- what the token is for
- what makes it different
- how it connects to SydTek University
- what the roadmap looks like

## Best Early-Member Strategy

Do not focus on mass traffic first.

Focus on:

- clear explanation
- strong onboarding
- repeatable message discipline
- direct community conversations

## First 100 Goal

The first 100 holders should feel like:

- founding community members
- early witnesses to the case study
- participants in a real system

## Rule

Strong early culture is more valuable than fast random reach.
