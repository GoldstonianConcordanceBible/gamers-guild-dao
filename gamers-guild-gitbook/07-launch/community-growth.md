# Community Growth Plan

Community growth should be intentional.

GGUILD should grow through trust, structure, and usefulness.

## Growth Priorities

The early focus should be:

- explanation
- onboarding
- consistency
- conversation
- repeated visibility

## Best Growth Channels

GGUILD can grow through:

- X
- Discord
- Telegram
- YouTube
- GitHub
- GitBook
- creator partnerships
- SydTek University program visibility

## Healthy Growth Model

A healthy community growth model looks like this:

1. people discover GGUILD
2. they read the docs
3. they understand the purpose
4. they join the channels
5. they stay because it feels real

## Community Building Tactics

Useful tactics include:

- pinned explanation posts
- regular roadmap updates
- contributor shout-outs
- simple onboarding threads
- screenshots of progress
- clear calls to read GitBook first

## What To Avoid

Avoid:

- spam replies
- fake urgency
- vague promises
- overposting without substance
- growth that cannot be onboarded properly

## Growth Principle

Trust scales slower than hype, but it lasts longer.
