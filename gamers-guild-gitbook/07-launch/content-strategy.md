# Content Strategy

Content is how GGUILD becomes understandable.

The project should not rely only on the token page. It needs a steady stream of clear public explanation.

## Content Goals

Content should:

- explain the project
- build trust
- show progress
- attract aligned users
- connect the DAO to SydTek University

## Best Content Types

Useful content includes:

- what GGUILD is
- why gaming needs better digital economies
- how AI agents fit into the system
- how GGUILD connects to the esports degree
- roadmap progress updates
- screenshots of GitBook and GitHub progress

## Weekly Content Rhythm

A simple rhythm could be:

- 1 explanation post
- 1 roadmap update
- 1 education-focused post
- 1 ecosystem or gaming thesis post

## Message Style

Keep the message:

- simple
- serious
- repeatable
- utility-focused
- community-centered

## Best Rule

Every piece of content should answer at least one of these questions:

- what is this
- why does it matter
- what is being built
- why should someone care

## Final Principle

Content should make the project easier to understand, not noisier.
