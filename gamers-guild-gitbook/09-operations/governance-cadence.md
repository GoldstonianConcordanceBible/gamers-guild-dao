# Governance Cadence

Governance should not happen only when there is chaos.

It should happen on a predictable rhythm.

## Why This Matters

A governance cadence helps the community understand when decisions are discussed, reviewed, and finalized.

## Example Cadence

### Week 1
Collect ideas and discussion topics.

### Week 2
Draft and refine proposals.

### Week 3
Open formal review and community feedback.

### Week 4
Vote and publish outcome.

## Benefits

A cadence makes governance:

- more understandable
- more transparent
- less reactive
- easier to document

## Principle

Even simple governance feels stronger when it follows a visible process.
