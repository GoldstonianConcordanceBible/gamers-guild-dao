# Operating Model

GGUILD needs more than a token and documentation.

It needs a way to operate consistently.

## Core Idea

The operating model defines how the project stays active, organized, and visible over time.

## What the Operating Model Covers

The operating model includes:

- contributor roles
- weekly activity
- governance flow
- reporting process
- community communication
- roadmap tracking

## Why This Matters

Many projects launch and then become quiet.

GGUILD should not depend on random bursts of energy.

It should operate through repeatable systems.

## Core Principle

A healthy project has:

- clear roles
- clear updates
- clear rhythm
- clear ownership of tasks

## GGUILD Operating Goal

The goal is to make the ecosystem feel alive, structured, and accountable.
