# Contributor Roles

A project becomes stronger when people know their role.

## Why Roles Matter

Roles reduce confusion and make it easier for contributors to help.

## Example Roles

### Builder
Works on infrastructure, systems, or implementation.

### Community Lead
Helps onboard users, answer questions, and keep discussion healthy.

### Creator
Makes content, clips, explanations, or visuals that help the ecosystem grow.

### Analyst
Tracks progress, metrics, dashboards, and performance patterns.

### Moderator
Supports order, clarity, and channel quality.

### Governance Contributor
Helps draft proposals, review ideas, and support decision-making processes.

### Research or University Contributor
Helps connect GGUILD to SydTek University coursework, case study materials, and documentation.

## Important Rule

Roles do not need to be overly formal at first.

But they do need to be clear enough that contributors know how to add value.
