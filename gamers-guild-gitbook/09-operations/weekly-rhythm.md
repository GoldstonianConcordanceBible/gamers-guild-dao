# Weekly Rhythm

A weekly rhythm helps GGUILD stay active and visible.

## Why This Matters

Without rhythm, the project can become reactive and inconsistent.

With rhythm, people know what to expect.

## Example Weekly Rhythm

### Monday
Roadmap and priorities update.

### Tuesday
Community discussion or education post.

### Wednesday
System or documentation progress update.

### Thursday
Creator or contributor spotlight.

### Friday
Weekly summary and KPI snapshot.

### Weekend
Light community engagement, planning, and review.

## Simple Rule

The exact days can change.

What matters is consistency.

## Goal

The weekly rhythm should make GGUILD feel like an active system, not a forgotten launch.
