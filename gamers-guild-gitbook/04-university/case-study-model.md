# Case Study Model

GGUILD is being built as a live case study.

That means the project is not only meant to operate. It is also meant to be documented, examined, and improved over time.

## What Makes It a Case Study

A real case study has:

- a documented mission
- a clear structure
- observable activity
- measurable outcomes
- a record of decisions
- lessons that can be taught

GGUILD is being structured around those elements.

## What Students and Researchers Can Study

They can study:

- how token utility is defined
- how communities respond to incentives
- how governance decisions are made
- how AI agents support operations
- how gaming ecosystems create value

## Why This Matters

This structure makes the project reusable.

Instead of disappearing after launch, GGUILD can keep generating:

- curriculum assets
- governance records
- research notes
- accreditation-supporting evidence
- future design improvements
