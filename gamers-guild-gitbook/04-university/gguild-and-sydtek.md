# GGUILD and SydTek University

Gamers Guild Token (GGUILD) is being developed as a Sub-DAO initiative within the SydTek University ecosystem.

This means GGUILD is not just a token launch. It is also part of a larger academic and institutional framework focused on Web3, gaming, AI, governance, and digital infrastructure.

## Why This Matters

Most token projects stop at launch.

GGUILD is different because it is being documented as:

- a live digital economy experiment
- a governance case study
- a gaming ecosystem model
- an academic project with long-term educational value

## Institutional Bridge

Gamers Guild DAO serves as the live digital economy case study layer for SydTek University’s B.S. in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance.

That line is the bridge between the token ecosystem and the university program.

## What SydTek Adds

SydTek University adds:

- academic credibility
- course integration
- case study value
- long-term documentation
- interdisciplinary learning opportunities

This makes GGUILD more than a community asset. It becomes a teaching and research asset.
