# Roadmap

The GGUILD roadmap is designed to move from concept to ecosystem through clear phases.

## Phase 1: Documentation

- finalize GitBook structure
- align GitHub, GitBook, and Pump page
- define utility, governance, and system language

## Phase 2: Community Activation

- grow Discord and Telegram
- onboard early contributors
- establish norms and messaging discipline

## Phase 3: Reputation and Rewards

- launch contribution tracking
- build reward logic
- recognize creators, organizers, and community builders

## Phase 4: Gaming Integration

- support tournament pilots
- test creator incentives
- explore guild-based participation models

## Phase 5: Governance Expansion

- formalize proposal templates
- open structured voting
- build decision records

## Phase 6: AI Operations

- deploy analytics and reporting agents
- automate selected workflows
- improve ecosystem intelligence

## Phase 7: SydTek University Integration

- connect GGUILD directly into coursework
- support case study assignments
- generate academic and assessment artifacts

## Phase 8: Scale and Refinement

- improve durability
- refine governance
- expand responsibly
- strengthen documentation and reporting
