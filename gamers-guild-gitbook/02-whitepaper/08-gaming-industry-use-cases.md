# Gaming Industry Use Cases

GGUILD is designed to support real functions inside gaming and gaming-adjacent communities.

The stronger the use cases, the stronger the ecosystem.

## Why Gaming Fits

Gaming communities already understand:

- rewards
- levels
- status
- contribution
- coordination
- digital identity

That makes gaming one of the most natural environments for a structured token ecosystem.

## Core Use Cases

GGUILD may support:

- contributor rewards
- creator incentives
- tournament participation
- community campaigns
- governance participation
- ecosystem access
- guild coordination

## Creator Use Cases

GGUILD can be used to recognize creators who add value through:

- videos
- clips
- commentary
- educational content
- community-building content

## Community Use Cases

GGUILD can support community activity through:

- engagement campaigns
- contribution recognition
- role-based participation
- access to selected opportunities

## Governance Use Cases

The token can also support structured participation in:

- voting
- proposal review
- ecosystem direction
- community priorities

## Final Point

Gaming use cases make GGUILD stronger because they connect the token to real behavior, not just abstract claims.
