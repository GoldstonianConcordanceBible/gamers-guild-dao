# Tokenomics

Tokenomics determines how the token is structured, distributed, and aligned with the long-term needs of the ecosystem.

## Why Tokenomics Matters

Bad tokenomics weakens the system.

Strong tokenomics helps support:

- contributor rewards
- ecosystem growth
- governance participation
- operational sustainability
- long-term clarity

## Core Tokenomics Areas

GGUILD tokenomics should define:

- total supply
- distribution logic
- ecosystem allocation
- contributor reward structure
- sustainability principles

## Design Goals

The tokenomics model should aim to be:

- transparent
- simple
- sustainable
- aligned with utility
- aligned with community participation

## What Tokenomics Should Support

Tokenomics should help support:

- community growth
- contributor recognition
- creator incentives
- responsible ecosystem development
- long-term participation

## What Tokenomics Should Avoid

Tokenomics should avoid becoming only a short-term attention mechanism.

The structure should not be built around hype alone.

## Final Point

The purpose of tokenomics is to strengthen the ecosystem over time, not just define supply on paper.
