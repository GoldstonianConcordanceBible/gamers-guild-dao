# AI and Reputation

GGUILD is designed to include both AI support systems and contribution-aware reputation systems.

These layers help the ecosystem become more intelligent, organized, and merit-aware.

## AI Layer

AI can help support GGUILD by improving:

- analytics
- reporting
- contribution tracking
- system visibility
- operational coordination

## Example AI Roles

Potential AI roles include:

- trend analysis
- contributor scoring support
- weekly reporting
- ecosystem monitoring
- dashboard updates

## Reputation Layer

A reputation system helps the project recognize people who contribute real value.

That may include people who help through:

- content creation
- moderation
- research
- community support
- ecosystem building

## Why Reputation Matters

Without a reputation layer, communities often overvalue noise and undervalue contribution.

A strong reputation system helps GGUILD:

- recognize real builders
- encourage healthy participation
- create better incentives
- support more meaningful governance

## Design Principle

AI should support the community, not replace it.

Reputation should recognize value, not create confusion.

## Final Point

Together, AI and reputation help GGUILD become more than a token. They help make it a smarter ecosystem.
