# Governance Model

GGUILD is designed to include governance as part of its long-term structure.

Governance is how the community moves from watching the project to helping shape it.

## Governance Purpose

The purpose of governance is to create a visible path for community participation in important ecosystem decisions.

## Governance Can Include

GGUILD governance may be used for:

- ecosystem priorities
- contributor initiatives
- community improvements
- feature ideas
- proposal review
- direction-setting decisions

## Governance Principles

Governance should be:

- understandable
- documented
- structured
- visible
- aligned with the mission of the project

## Why Governance Matters

Without governance, community energy stays passive.

With governance, the community can help shape:

- what gets built
- what gets prioritized
- how the ecosystem evolves

## Practical Principle

Governance does not need to be overly complicated at the beginning.

It needs to be clear enough for people to trust and participate in.

## Final Point

The governance model should help GGUILD become more community-centered over time while still remaining structured and readable.
