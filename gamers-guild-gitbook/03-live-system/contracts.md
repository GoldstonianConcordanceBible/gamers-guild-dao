# Smart Contracts

Smart contracts are the foundation of the GGUILD token.

## What They Do

Smart contracts control:

- token supply
- transfers
- minting rules
- basic permissions

## Why They Matter

They make the system:

- transparent
- verifiable
- consistent

## GGUILD Design Approach

The contract layer is designed to be:

- simple
- secure
- auditable
- limited in scope

## Important Note

Smart contracts handle the token itself.

They do not control:

- community behavior
- governance discussions
- reputation scoring
- AI logic

Those happen in other layers.
