# Do Not Say

Do not describe GGUILD as:

- guaranteed profit
- guaranteed upside
- passive income
- risk free
- safe investment
- SEC approved
- White House approved
- government approved
- guaranteed moon
- certain 100x
- financial freedom token

## Do Not Say These Types of Sentences

Do not say:

- buyers will make money from our work
- regulators approved this
- this is compliant because we said so
- this is not a security as a final legal conclusion
- the team will pump the token
- this token will definitely appreciate

## Rule

Describe what the token does.

Do not make promises about price, profit, or approval.
